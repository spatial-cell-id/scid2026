Note: The sessions **"Round Table with Experts / Q&A / Bring Your Own Data"** are designed as an interactive space for summer school students to engage directly with specialists in spatial transcriptomics data analysis. Participants are encouraged to discuss their specific research projects, address technical bottlenecks, and troubleshoot practical challenges encountered during their own data analysis with the support of experts in the field.
