### About the Summer School
The SCID 2026 Summer School focuses on using Spatial OMICs to uncover cell identity during developmental processes. The program combines theoretical lectures with hands-on bioinformatics sessions.

### Target Audience
This school is designed for researchers, PhD students, and engineers (IR/IE) working in bioinformatics, genomics, and developmental biology who wish to expand their skills in spatial transcriptomics data analysis. 

### Prerequisites
* Basic knowledge of R or Python.
* Familiarity with standard single-cell RNA-seq analysis workflows.
* Attendees are required to bring their own laptops.
