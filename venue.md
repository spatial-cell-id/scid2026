The First Spatial-Cell-ID Summer School will be held in the beautiful alpine village of Aussois, France. 

### The Conference Center
The event takes place at the **Centre Paul Langevin**, a facility accustomed to hosting scientific workshops and conferences in a relaxed and focused environment.

* **Address:** 24 Rue du Coin, 73500 Aussois, France

### How to Get There

**By Train:**
The closest railway station is **Modane**, which is well-connected to Lyon.

The SNCF route planner/reservation system has not yet been updated with the train connection between Lyon and Modane (via Chambéry). 
You can download the plan <a href="/assets/images/fiche_horaire.pdf" download>here</a>


**Shuttle Bus / Taxi:**
We will organize a dedicated shuttle bus from Modane station to the conference center.
- Outbound: Departs Modane at 4:30 PM.
- Return: Departs Aussois around 1:00 PM.

*If you arrive outside of the shuttle schedule, local taxis are available at the station.*

**By Plane:**
The nearest major airports are:
* Paris Airports (CDG/ORY): You can reach Chambéry from Paris via high-speed train (TGV) from the station located directly at Paris-Charles de Gaulle (CDG) or by connecting through central Paris stations.
* Lyon-Saint Exupéry (LYS)
