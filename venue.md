The First Spatial-Cell-ID Summer School will be held in the beautiful alpine village of Aussois, France. 

### The Conference Center
The event takes place at the **Centre Paul Langevin**, a facility accustomed to hosting scientific workshops and conferences in a relaxed and focused environment.

* **Address:** 24 Rue du Coin, 73500 Aussois, France
* **Website:** [Inserire link al centro se disponibile]

### How to Get There

**By Train:**
The closest railway station is **Chambéry**, which is well-connected by direct trains from both Paris and Lyon.

_Recommended outbound journeys_:
From Lyon: TER 18517 departs Lyon Part-Dieu at 14:50 and arrives at Chambéry–Challes-les-Eaux at 16:16.
From Paris: TGV INOUI 6941 departs Paris Gare de Lyon at 12:46 and arrives at Chambéry–Challes-les-Eaux at 15:36.

_Recommended return journeys_:
To Lyon: TER 18517 departs Chambéry–Challes-les-Eaux at 15:44 and arrives at Lyon Part-Dieu at 17:10.
To Paris: TGV INOUI 6978 departs Chambéry–Challes-les-Eaux at 16:23 and arrives at Paris Gare de Lyon at 19:14.

**Shuttle Bus / Taxi:**
We will organize a dedicated shuttle bus from Chambéry station to the conference center.
- Outbound: Departs Chambéry at 4:30 PM (arrival in Aussois around 6:30 PM).
- Return: Departs Aussois around 1:00 PM (arrival in Chambéry to meet the recommended trains).

*If you arrive outside of the shuttle schedule, local taxis are available at the station.*

**By Plane:**
The nearest major airports are:
* Paris Airports (CDG/ORY): You can reach Chambéry from Paris via high-speed train (TGV) from the station located directly at Paris-Charles de Gaulle (CDG) or by connecting through central Paris stations.
* Lyon-Saint Exupéry (LYS)
* Geneva (GVA)
* Turin (TRN)

From these airports, you can easily reach Chambéry station by train.
